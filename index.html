Idaho's Women of Influence is a project meant to correct an imbalance in the preserved historical data on women's contributions to Idaho history.

IWI is open to women connected to Idaho by birth, residence, work, or any other significant factor, whose positive efforts and accomplishments contributed significantly to quality of life in their community, the state, or the world. The collection provides vetted, publicly available descriptions and resources: browse the biographies to explore Idaho history or download the metadata to jump start new projects.
